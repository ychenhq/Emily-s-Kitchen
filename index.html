    For all ages!

  • Tools You Definitely Need

      -Measuring Cups (Liquid and Dry) and Spoons
      -Rubber Spatula/Scraper
      -Pastry Brush
      -Whisk
      -Kitchen Scissors
      -Rolling Pin
      -Fine-Mesh Sieve
      -Wire Rack
      -Baking Sheet/Cookie Sheet/Sheet Pan
      -Parchment Paper
      -Differnt Shaped Molds

      Sometimes it is easier to have less tools unless you're a pro.
  • Pre-Heat the Oven

      Make sure you pre-heat the oven at least ten minutes before you hand in your unbaked ingredients! Else things will end up unexpectedly!

  • Do Not Panic

      It is always true that beginners tend to follow every step for fear of failures. However, there are times that things go off track. Keep in mind that even proffesionals make mistakes constantly. Unless you burnt down your house, any mistake is tolerable. Just clean up the mess and try once again!

    Cinnamon Rolls
    Ingredients:
    Dough:
    All-purpose flour 300g
    Milk 205g
    Butter 52g
    Sugar 40g
    Yeast 3.1g
    Salt
    Stuffings:
    Brown sugar 68g
    Ground Cinnamon 5g (or the way you like)
    Cream cheese (Optional)
    Apple Sauce (Optional) Butter 78g
    Decoration:
    Powdered Sugar 80g
    Water 20g
    Steps:

    1. Make the dough from the ingredients listed above. Gently fold the dough and cover it with plastic wrap for ferment. Place the dough at the warmest spot you can find indoors.

    2. Start makeing the stuffings. Add the melted butter with ground cinnamon, mix it until well blended. Highly recommend those who have tried multiple times to add some apple sauce, it elevates the flavor suprisingly!

    3. After finishing the stuffings, take the dough out, and make sure it's rested for at least 1 hour. The dough should be at least twice as large. Fold the dough and release the bubbles contained.

    4. Shape the dough in a 35*25*2 (length, width, and height), remember not to make the dough too dry while shaping. Then spread the stuffings on to the dough.

    5. Roll the dough as if you're rolling paper but extremely gentle. Rest it on the oven plate for secondary ferment. In the meantime, pre-heat the oven at 180 degrees.

    6. After 30 minutes, bake the dough for 25-35 minutes. After resting in room temperature, add the decoration on top of your well-made cinnamon rolls!

    Additional imformation can be found at this link: (Perfect Homemade Cinnamon Rolls Recipe, No Mixer. ASMR)

    New York CheeseCake
    Ingredients:
    Cream cheese 500mg
    Egg Yolk *2
    Milk 50mg
    Sugar 7TB
    Greek Yogurt 60mg
    Lemon juice 15mg
    Gelatin 10mg
    Butter 15mg
    Steps:

    1. Whip the cream cheese, add 4 tablespoons of sugar gradually and separately. Afterwards, mix the soft cream cheese with 4 TB of sugar evenly.

    2. Mix the Yolk with 3TB of sugar. Stir it until the color turn light yellow. Then, add the yolks with the cream cheese.

    3. Add greek yogurt to the whole mixture, and add melted gelatin and butter. Gently fold the mixture until it is fully mixed. Put it into the mold ready to bake.

    4. Pre-heat the oven at 150 degrees. After 10 minutes, put the mold into the oven and bake it for 35 minutes.

    Raspberry CheeseCake
    Ingredients:
    Cream cheese 500mg
    Heavy cream 250mg
    Sugar 7TB
    Lemon juice 15mg
    Strawberry *7
    Raspberry*15
    Gelatin 10mg
    Chocolate chips 25mg
    Digestive cookies 7 pieces
    Butter 15mg
    Steps:

    1. Smash 6-7 digestive cookies and add 20mg butter to make the lower crust of the cake. Spread the cookies on the mould and put it in the fridge.

    2. Whip the heavy cream, add 4 tablespoons of sugar gradually and separately. To make it easier to be whipped, add a little lemon juice while whipping. Afterwards, mix the soft cream cheese with sugar evenly and add the whipped cream to make the cheesecake base.

    3. Cut the strawberries in half, and place it on the edges, then add the cheesecake mixture into the mold. Place it in the fridge to make the second chocolate flavor layer.

    4. Repeat the steps above of making the cheesecake mixture, this time, add melted chocolate. Take the mold out of the fridge, and spread it evenly as the second layer.

    5. Grind the raspberries with a grinder (add 10mg of water), and add 1stb of sugar. Spread it on top of the cheesecake, then put it in the freezer.

    Ocean Cake
    Ingredients:
    300ml Heavy Cream
    2 egg yolks
    5TB sugar
    2 mangos
    7 digestive cookies
    15mg butter
    1 drop of blue food coloring
    40mg gelatin
    Steps:

    1. Smash 4-5 digestive cookies and add 20mg butter to make the lower crust of the cake. Spread the cookies on the mould and put it in the fridge.

    2. Whip the heavy cream, add 4 tablespoons of sugar gradually and separately.

    3. Mix the yolks with 1TB sugar, and add it to the whipping cream you made.

    4. Cut the mangos into small cubes. If the mangos are too sour, white chocolate (20grams) are suggested in adding to balance the sourness. Afterwards, gently crush half of the cubes and mix it with the melted white chocolate along with a little milk.

    5. Mix the whipping cream with the mango gooey and the cubes, gently fold them until it’s mixed. Add the melted gelatin 20grams into the mixture to make it solid.

    6. Pour the mixture into the mould. Freeze it and start making the blue gelatin ocean toppings.

    7. Add 15 grams of melted gelatin + 100ml of cold water + 1-2 drips of blue food coloring. Take out the mould from the freezer, pour 50 grams on top to freeze. Reserve the rest for making the waves, put them in the fridge to let it cook down as jelly.

    8. After 12 hours in the freezer, and 5 hours in the fridge, take the mould and the rest of jelly out. Crush the rest of the jelly into big and small pieces to create waves, and spread it on the top. Crush another digestive cookie to make the beach next to the waves

    Tiramisu
    Ingredients:
    300ml Heavy Cream
    Egg yolks *4
    Heavy cream 250g
    Mill 60g 5TB sugar
    Coffee Extract 100mg
    7 digestive cookies
    15mg butter
    Gelatin 25mg
    Steps:

    Tiramisu Recipe 1. Stir the egg yolks and add 1-2 TB of sugar. Stir until sugar is well mixed.

    2. Melt the gelatin and mix it with the yolks. Since the gelatin is melted in hot water, the yolks will be light yellow.

    3. Beat the heavy cream until it is 80% whipped, in the mean time, add around 4 TB of sugar gradually (appx 3 times individually) while stirring.

    4. Add the yolks with whipped heavy cream. While mixing these two ingredients, add 60 grams of milk and 25 grams of butter into the mixture. It flavors the taste pretty well.

    5. Afterwards, make the crust with digestive cookies. In order to make the crust flavorful, dip the whole cookie into a small plate with coffee+1TB sugar+ butter. Lady fingers are commonly seen but in my opinion digestive cookies are better choices.

    6. Evenly stuff the dipped cookies in the mould. In most cases, people crush it and add butter to make it taste crunchy. Either way should do!

    7. Lastly, if the inner stuffings , the yolk and whipped heavy cream in particular, is more liquidity , then a non leaking mould would be suggested. A better way is to put plastic wrap around the mold to prevent leakage. On the other hand, while spreading the coookies, I’d add a second layer of crust in the middle for a better taste.

    8. Put the cake into the freezer for 24 hours without the lid, which wavers a huge part of water. Then move it to the fridge, store it for another 5 hours, then the cake should be ready!
