Steps:

1. Whip the cream cheese, add 4 tablespoons of sugar gradually and separately. Afterwards, mix the soft cream cheese with 4 TB of sugar evenly.

2. Mix the Yolk with 3TB of sugar. Stir it until the color turn light yellow. Then, add the yolks with the cream cheese.

3. Add greek yogurt to the whole mixture, and add melted gelatin and butter. Gently fold the mixture until it is fully mixed. Put it into the mold ready to bake.

4. Pre-heat the oven at 150 degrees. After 10 minutes, put the mold into the oven and bake it for 35 minutes.
